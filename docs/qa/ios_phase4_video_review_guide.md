# iOS Phase 4 Video Review Guide

Record landscape video for at least these flows:

1. Launch to title.
2. Tap game start.
3. Return to title.
4. Open character select, shop, collection, achievements, settings, help.
5. Rotate landscape-left to landscape-right if the device allows it.
6. Play 10 minutes in iOS low profile.
7. Visit at least two biome regions.

Review criteria:

- No clipped title buttons.
- No unreadable Japanese text.
- No UI hidden under device unsafe areas.
- Environment texture improves place identity without reducing combat readability.
- No new audio.
- No obvious frame pacing collapse.
